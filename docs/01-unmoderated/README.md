# Unmoderated Content

Unmoderated content is low-risk content that can publish after automated categorization and baseline safety checks. It is still subject to reporting, abuse controls, and post-publication moderation.

## Allowed Here

- Ordinary chat and user-to-user intercommunication.
- Personal status posts and comments that do not claim official authority.
- Leisure reading lists and non-professional book notes.
- Draft personal reflections that are not used as policy, legal, academic, or professional material.

## Not Allowed Here

- Laws, policies, contracts, compliance guidance, or governance rules.
- Professional instruction or certification material.
- Academic material used for formal learning paths.
- Medical, financial, safety, or legal advice.
