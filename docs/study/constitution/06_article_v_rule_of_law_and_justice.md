## Article V: Rule of Law and Justice

1. Laws must be public, stable, knowable, and equally applied.
2. Retroactive penal law is prohibited.
3. Collective punishment is prohibited.
4. Independent courts or equivalent tribunals must remain free from political and financial capture.
5. Every person has access to remedy for rights violations.
6. Public institutions are liable for unlawful harm caused by negligence, abuse, or unconstitutional action.
