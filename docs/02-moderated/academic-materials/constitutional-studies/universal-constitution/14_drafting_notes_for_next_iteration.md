## Drafting Notes for Next Iteration

For Version 0.2, resolve these design choices explicitly:

1. Membership model: citizenship, residency, and non-citizen protections.
2. Voting architecture: representative, direct, or hybrid constitutional model.
3. Institutional map: exact bodies, term lengths, and appointment method.
4. Rights enforceability: direct court action vs staged administrative remedy.
5. Economic baseline guarantees: minimum service floor and funding rules.
6. Emergency threshold definitions and time caps.
7. Constitutional review cadence and revision trigger conditions.
