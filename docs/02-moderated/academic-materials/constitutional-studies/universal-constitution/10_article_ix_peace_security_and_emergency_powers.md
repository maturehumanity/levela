## Article IX: Peace, Security, and Emergency Powers

1. The primary purpose of security institutions is protection of life, rights, and constitutional order.
2. Emergency powers are temporary, necessary, and proportionate only.
3. Emergency declarations must include:
   - legal basis,
   - objective criteria,
   - scope limits,
   - expiration date,
   - independent oversight.
4. Core non-derogable rights remain protected at all times.
5. All emergency actions are automatically reviewable and publishable after operational risk has passed.
