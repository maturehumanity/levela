# Advisory and High Council Model v0.1

## 1. Purpose

Levela should eventually establish a high advisory and constitutional body that supports legitimacy, review, continuity, and broad representation without collapsing into one-person rule.

## 2. Nature

This body should be neither purely symbolic nor all-powerful. It should exist to review high-level constitutional matters, activation questions, integrity concerns, reserved-domain ratification issues, and emergency legitimacy questions.

## 3. Suggested Membership

Later membership may include:

- Founder
- constitutional reviewers
- high-trust civic representatives
- domain specialists where relevant

## 4. Principle

The council must itself remain procedurally limited, auditable, and non-dictatorial. It exists to stabilize Levela's constitutional order, not to replace one form of central domination with another.
