# Governance Permission Model v0.1

## 1. Rule

No governance permission may be granted unless the person is verified, governance-eligible, in good standing, and above the required Levela score threshold for that permission class.

## 2. Permission Classes

- Member permissions
- Verified permissions
- Citizen permissions
- Active citizen permissions
- Governance permissions

Only governance-eligible citizens may exercise governance permissions.

## 3. Governance Tiers

- Tier G1: Basic governance
  - examples: ordinary voting, formal civic discussion
  - suggested minimum score: 70

- Tier G2: Proposal and review governance
  - examples: proposal sponsorship, ordinary governance review
  - suggested minimum score: 75

- Tier G3: Constitutional and high-trust governance
  - examples: constitutional review, treasury oversight, identity appeals, activation review
  - suggested minimum score: 80 or 85 depending on risk

## 4. Fairness Principle

Score is mandatory for entry into governance, but should not automatically multiply ordinary voting power. The recommended default remains one eligible citizen equaling one vote unless a narrower constitutional exception is later defined.
