# Country Activation Framework v0.1

## 1. Purpose

This framework defines when Levela internally activates its civic system for a country or for the world.

## 2. Activation Principle

Levela may recognize activation when enough verified citizens of a jurisdiction have joined and accepted Levela's civic framework. Activation should be treated as an internal constitutional event, not as a casual product metric.

## 3. Tentative Threshold

The current planning threshold is 51 percent of a country's verified citizens, or 51 percent of verified world citizens for world-level activation, subject to later demographic and constitutional review.

## 4. Stages

- Pre-activation
- Pending activation review
- Activated

## 5. Effects

Upon activation, relevant citizens become Active automatically inside Levela. Levela's internal civic framework then comes into force for participating Levela citizens within the system.

## 6. Review Requirement

Activation should require demographic verification review, constitutional review, technical integrity review, and a public audit trail before final recognition.
