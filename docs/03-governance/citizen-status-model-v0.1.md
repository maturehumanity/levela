# Citizen Status Model v0.1

## 1. Status Layers

Levela should distinguish among:

1. Registered Member
2. Verified Member
3. Citizen
4. Active Citizen
5. Governance-Eligible Citizen

## 2. Registered Member

A registered member has signed up and may use ordinary member features, but has no governance rights.

## 3. Verified Member

A verified member has passed identity and contact verification. Verification strengthens trust standing but does not itself create citizenship or governance power.

## 4. Citizen

A citizen is a verified member who has remained signed up long enough to be reviewed and who has accepted Levela's internal civic framework. Citizenship may arise after 30 days automatically or after 14 days through eligible manual acceptance.

## 5. Active Citizen

An active citizen is a citizen whose civic standing becomes active because the relevant internal Levela activation threshold has been recognized.

## 6. Governance-Eligible Citizen

A governance-eligible citizen is an active, verified citizen who also satisfies the minimum Levela score, good-standing, device trust, and any required training or certification needed for governance rights.

## 7. Badge Principle

Verified, Citizen, Active, and Governance Eligible should remain separate badges so that users and institutions can understand status clearly. Founder, where shown, should be a distinct office badge rather than a replacement for these civic states.
